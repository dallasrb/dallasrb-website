h3. A Journey through Time, Date, DateTime and TimeWithZone

Wade Winningham will guide us on a trip through the recesses of how Ruby and Rails (ActiveSupport) helps us deal with dates, times and time zones.

h3. ActiveSupport MiniSeries - Class Attribute

We'll kickoff a new miniseries on ActiveSupport by taking a look at class_attribute.

h3. RubyNews

Karthik Hariharan will keep us up to date with the latest news for Ruby and Rails.

h3. Meeting Open to Everyone

We welcome everyone to the Dallas.rb meetings.  Come out and ask questions, help answer questions, or just be a part of the conversation.  There is always something new to learn!
